# Calibration — adaptive difficulty v2 (obstacle injection)

Date: 2026-07-04 · Method: `npx tsx scripts/probe-adaptive.ts 300` (300 greedy runs per level per tier; deterministic — same variant scheme as simulateLevel with applyTierTo applied per-variant inside the loop, so injection stays per-seed deterministic). Floor: win >= 0.40 at tier +2; decreases monotonic-ish with tier.

## Injection constants (final, decision #24 full form)

| tier | moves | ice injected | boxes injected |
|---|---|---|---|
| -2 / -1 | +2 / +1 | 0 | 0 (v1 behavior, board untouched) |
| 0 | 0 | 0 | 0 |
| +1 | -1 | 3 | 0 |
| +2 | -2 | 5 | 1 (hp1) |

Injection fires only on all-'collect'-goal levels (obstacle-goal economies never distorted — those fall back to moves-only). Seeded rng from `level.seed * 31 + tier`. Guards, never violated (skip instead): total boxes <= floor(w*h/9); movable (non-box) cells >= 2/3; ice only on '.' cells; authored b/B/i always preserved.

## Probe results (greedy, 300 runs)

Only kitchen has all-collect levels (001-010 layoutless, 012 with authored boxes) — dance/gym/vanity all carry obstacle goals and get the moves-only fallback by design. Probed 002 (simple short), 005 (two goals, shortest budget — worst case), 012 (long budget; its 6 authored boxes exceed the cap of 4, so the +2 box is guard-skipped — exercises the guard path).

| level | tier 0 win | tier +1 win | tier +2 win | gift% (0 → +2) |
|---|---|---|---|---|
| kitchen-002 | 92.7% | 89.3% | 84.7% | 36.0 → 62.3 |
| kitchen-005 | 89.3% | 84.7% | 74.3% | 61.7 → 81.7 |
| kitchen-012 | 84.0% | 79.7% | 75.7% | 46.7 → 61.7 |

All monotonic decreasing; worst case (005 at +2) 74.3%, far above the 0.40 floor — no constant reduction needed. Consistent with the lever notes: ice is the gentlest obstacle, and +/-2 moves on 7-10-move budgets does most of the work; the injected obstacles add texture without cliffing. Gift usage rises sharply with tier (005 hits 81.7% at +2) — accepted: tier +2 players are sustained 3-star performers and the gift is the intended pressure valve.

## Notes

- Probe is repeatable and self-checking: exits 1 if any level dips below the floor at +2.
- avgMoves can exceed the tiered budget (giftMoves extend play) — expected.
- These numbers measure the greedy policy on kitchen collect levels; adaptive tiers on obstacle-goal levels remain moves-only and are covered by the original per-level calibration bands.
