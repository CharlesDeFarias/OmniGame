**Goal:** Dance studio, gym, and vanity/makeup chapters (decisions #32/#38): chapter system with influencer-level unlocks, 30 new calibrated levels, a music piece-pack for the dance chapter (pack mechanism per decision #9), the fashion wardrobe coin sink (#36), and the plan-6 carry-forward fixes.

**Fixed design:**
- Chapters in order: kitchen (exists) → dance (unlocks at influencer level 3) → gym (level 4) → vanity (level 5). Locked chapters show dimmed in the strip with a level badge; tapping an unlocked chapter switches the career room view and makes its levels the active set.
- Each chapter: 10 levels (dance levels/dance/021-030.json, gym levels/gym/031-040.json, vanity levels/vanity/041-050.json — ids dance-021 etc.), its own 6-slot room, its own film-a-video milestone (same flow, room-appropriate payout ×1.25 per chapter index rounded).
- Rooms (6 slots each): dance = mirror, barre, speaker, discoball, mat, poster. gym = treadmill, weights, bench, fan, mat, chart. vanity = mirror-lights, chair, desk, rack, ringlight, shelf. Prices per chapter tuned by ECONOMY PROBE (target: room completable around level 8-9 of its chapter at 2-star pace, leaving 15-25% leftover for wardrobe; adjust per-chapter coin bonus if needed: coinsPerWin may gain +chapterIndex*5 — decide by probe, document in calibration doc).
- Piece packs: `src/render/packs.ts` — PackId 'gems' (default) | 'music' (dance chapter): music pack = 6 NEW procedural shapes, same 6 colors (accessibility: color + distinct shape preserved): note, vinyl disc, mic, headphones, star, speaker-cone. textureKeyFor becomes pack-aware (`{pack}-{color}`); gym/vanity reuse gems this plan (real per-chapter art = plan 7).
- Wardrobe (fashion sink): `src/meta/wardrobe.ts` catalog — 6 outfits (outfit colors beyond the base 3; prices 80/100/120/150/180/220) + `src/services/wardrobe.ts` (owned, equipped; buy via wallet.spend). Career screen gets a wardrobe button (hanger icon) → shop overlay (avatar previews, buy/equip). Equipped outfit drives video-moment avatar defaults and dance-break avatar.
- Progress v2: `{version:2, chapter, levelIndexByChapter: Record<chapter, number>, completed, stars}` with silent v1 migration (v1 levelIndex → kitchen; chapter 'kitchen'). Chapter completion → career; next chapter's levels only when its room's chapter is ACTIVE (player picks chapter in career; play button plays active chapter's next level; finished chapters replayable via their strip icon).
- Boot rule fix (carry-forward): boot to career whenever ANY progress exists (any completed level or coins > 0), not levelIndex>0.
- Carry-forward fixes: remove dead `avatar-0`; remove dead goalHud.color; parent-corner per-level list becomes two columns (10 rows each) to fit 50 levels... 50 rows won't fit — switch per-level rows to a compact grid: 5 columns × N rows of 'NNN★' (level number + bestStars as tiny gold stars count), rowstep 40.
